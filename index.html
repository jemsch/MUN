Who we are

The Design Tech High School Model United Nations team is a highly competitive team of 23 dedicated students. We make up the honors class International Studies: Debate and Diplomacy and are coached by veteran MUNer and English teacher Rachel Siegman.

  • December 1-2: San Francisco Model United Nations (SFMUN)
  • March 3-5: Berkeley Model United Nations (BMUN)
  • May 18-19: Davis Model United Nations (DMUN)
